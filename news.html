Who is the Caesar salad
named after?
Caesar salad has nothing to do with Julius Caesar. It is named after Caesar Cardini, an American chef of Italian descent, who invented the recipe. According to legend, Cardini made a salad from what was left in the kitchen of his restaurant when, during the celebration of US Independence Day, there was nothing left from the menu, and customers demanded food.

Making a Caesar salad is also easy. Wash lettuce leaves, dry and tear into small pieces. Add croutons, pour dressing and mix. Sprinkle with cheese. Stir once more and serve immediately. If desired, you can decorate the dish with halved cherry tomatoes and half a boiled egg.

Appearance of summer terraces
no longer needs to be coordinated
Since the autumn of 2016, a document has been in force, according to which it was necessary to coordinate the appearance of both non-stationary catering facilities and the so-called remote summer areas. Changes in this activity happened in favor of restaurateurs. The Prosecutor General's Office considered that such practice is illegal.

Belarusian legislation does not stipulate the fact that business entities are obliged to coordinate design projects of how the summer terrace will look like in the executive authorities.

How can I order pastries for Easter?
Cool bakeries and confectioneries have instilled in Minsk residents a delicious tradition of pre-ordering Easter pastries. In Rosa Cafe, you can order products for every taste, from standard Easter cakes to fragrant panettones, which are prepared for at least 48 hours.

This year, the cafe offers two options: Easter cakes weighing 1.2 kg and 0.7 kg. The institution emphasizes that they are prepared exclusively from natural ingredients: tender dough with the addition of candied fruit and raisins. Decorated with white and black Belgian chocolate. Large Easter cakes are decorated with almonds, hazelnuts, cashews and walnuts, as well as a scattering of dried fruits (figs, cherries, prunes and dates).
